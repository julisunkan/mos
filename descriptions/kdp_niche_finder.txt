DESCRIPTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

KDP Niche & Keyword Finder is an AI-powered market research tool that helps
Amazon KDP publishers discover profitable publishing niches, evaluate competition
levels, and identify the exact keywords buyers are using to find books in any
topic area.

Instead of spending hours manually searching Amazon, guessing at trends, or
paying for expensive third-party keyword tools, users type a single topic or
idea and get back a comprehensive research report in seconds. The AI analyses
the market, identifies 5–8 distinct sub-niches within that topic, scores each
one for opportunity and competition, provides estimated monthly search volumes
and average Best Seller Rank (BSR) ranges, and lists 10 keyword phrases per
niche plus 15–20 top-level keywords ranked by opportunity score.

Every niche card includes:
  • Competition rating (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
  • Opportunity score (1–10)
  • Estimated monthly searches
  • Average BSR range
  • Price range for successful titles
  • Trend direction (Growing / Stable / Declining)
  • Content types that sell well (journals, workbooks, planners, etc.)
  • 5 potential book title ideas
  • Target audience description
  • An insider tip with a specific, actionable recommendation

The top-keywords section provides a ranked list of phrases with competition
ratings, opportunity scores, average selling prices, and guidance on whether
each keyword belongs in the title, subtitle, or keyword slots.

Quick wins and books-to-avoid sections provide strategic guidance, and the
seasonal trends section highlights demand patterns throughout the year.

All research is saved to the database. Full results can be exported as a CSV
file for use in spreadsheets, team sharing, or long-term planning.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FEATURES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  1. Niche discovery — identifies 5–8 distinct sub-niches within any topic with
     full market analysis for each.

  2. Opportunity scoring — each niche and keyword receives a numerical
     opportunity score (1–10) based on demand vs. competition.

  3. Competition analysis — HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW rating for both niches and
     individual keywords.

  4. Keyword research — 10 keywords per niche plus 15–20 global top keywords,
     with monthly search estimates and placement recommendations.

  5. BSR estimation — estimated average Best Seller Rank range to gauge how
     competitive a niche is in practice.

  6. Price range data — recommended retail price range based on what is
     selling in the niche.

  7. Trend indicator — Growing / Stable / Declining trend for each niche.

  8. Content type suggestions — identifies which book formats perform best
     (lined journal, workbook, planner, tracker, coloring book, etc.).

  9. Title ideas — 5 ready-to-use potential book title ideas per niche.

 10. Target audience profiles — clear description of the ideal reader for
     each niche.

 11. Insider tips — specific, actionable recommendations per niche based on
     current market conditions.

 12. Quick wins — 3 immediately actionable recommendations for the overall topic.

 13. Books-to-avoid — warns about over-saturated sub-niches to steer clear of.

 14. Seasonal trends — insights into how demand shifts throughout the year.

 15. CSV export — download the full research report as a structured CSV file
     with separate sections for niches, keywords, quick wins, and more.

 16. Search history — last 10 searches shown on the homepage; last 30 in the
     admin history view.

 17. Admin panel — track total searches, configure Groq API key and admin
     password.

 18. User report system — flag content issues; admins review and respond.

 19. PWA support — installable as a Progressive Web App.
