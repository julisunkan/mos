DESCRIPTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Legal Risk Checker is an AI-powered document analysis tool that helps
individuals, freelancers, small business owners, and legal professionals
instantly identify dangerous, unfair, or potentially harmful clauses inside
contracts, agreements, and legal documents — without needing a lawyer.

Users upload a PDF, DOCX, DOC, or TXT file and the system extracts the full
text, sends it to a large language model (Llama 3.3 70B via Groq), and receives
a structured risk analysis broken down by clause. Every flagged clause is
categorised by risk level (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW), risk type (Liability,
Termination, Non-Compete, Arbitration, IP Rights, Penalty, etc.), a detailed
explanation of why it is dangerous, and a concrete, actionable recommendation
for how to address it.

The overall document receives a top-level risk rating and a plain-English
executive summary. Results can be downloaded as a professionally formatted PDF
report suitable for sharing with lawyers, clients, or business partners.

The app keeps a searchable history of all documents analysed and allows
administrators to configure the Groq API key, file size limits, and admin
password through a protected settings panel.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FEATURES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  1. Multi-format upload — supports PDF, DOCX, DOC, and TXT files (up to
     configurable MB limit, default 10 MB).

  2. AI-powered clause detection — uses Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model to scan up
     to 12,000 characters of document text and return a structured JSON analysis.

  3. Risk level classification — each flagged clause is rated HIGH, MEDIUM, or
     LOW with colour-coded indicators.

  4. Risk type categorisation — clauses are tagged with types such as:
     Liability, Termination, Penalty, IP Rights, Non-Compete, Arbitration,
     Indemnification, Confidentiality, Jurisdiction, Auto-Renewal, and more.

  5. Detailed explanations — for every risky clause the AI explains why it is
     problematic in plain English.

  6. Actionable recommendations — specific guidance on how to negotiate, remove,
     or modify each clause.

  7. Overall risk rating — document-level rating (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) plus a
     2–3 sentence executive summary.

  8. Downloadable PDF report — professionally formatted report with colour-coded
     risk indicators, ready for sharing or filing.

  9. Document history — last 20 analysed documents shown on the dashboard with
     status (pending / analysed) and overall risk.

 10. Admin dashboard — view total documents, analysed count, and high-risk count.
     Configure Groq API key, admin password, and max file size.

 11. User report system — users can flag content issues; admins can review and
     approve or decline reports.

 12. PWA support — installable as a Progressive Web App with standalone display
     and custom icon.
